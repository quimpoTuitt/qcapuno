Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Note: You can also call 0916-3622876 for your questions and inquiries.

Reservation

Q: How to reserve the place and book the dates?

A: Please call our hotline number: 09163622876. In order for us to confirm your reservation, we require 50% down payment through bank deposit. Bank account details shall only be given to sure clients through text. Payment of the balance (other 50%) is upon check in.

Accomodation

Q: What are the rates?

A: It’s 15,000 on weekdays,/ 20,000 on weekends and holidays per overnight stay good for 15-20 people. Mondays to Thursdays are on weekdays rate, Fridays to Sunday and holidays are on weekend rate.

Low season promo rate is 20% off which is from August to September only. It's12,000 on weekdays, and 16,000 on weekends and holidays per night for overnight stay good for 15-20 people. Mondays to Thursdays are on weekday rate and Fridays to Sundays and holidays are on weekend rate.

Q: What is the maximum capacity of the beach house?

A: The rates and accommodation of the beach house are really good for 15-20 pax. However, we have a provision for up to 25 pax. There’s additional charge if exceeding 20 pax, 500 per head. Kids are included in the head count. Only 2 years old and below are free. We can provide extra mattresses free of charge. Cannot do more than 25 pax.

Q: What time is the check in and check out?

A: We follow a standard time: Check in at 2 PM Check out at 12 Noon. Early check in is not allowed. Late check out (up to 2 PM only) depends on the availability.

Ameneties

Q: Do you have a pool?

A: Sorry, no pool as of now. It is a beachfront beach house private property.

Q: Can we cook?

A: Absolutely, yes! There’s an equipped kitchen at the beach house so you can cook and bring your own food. No corkage fee. Equipped kitchen includes: refrigerator, rice cooker, stove, toaster, water dispenser, and outdoor grill. We provide basic kitchen utensils such as plates, spoons, and forks, and cooking wares like regular-sized pots and pans. Please bring your own disposable ones to be sure such as paper plates, cups, and plastic spoons and forks, etc.

Location

Q: Where is it located? Do you provide directions and maps going there?

A: Sea Breeze beach house is located along Laiya beach, Barangay Hugom, San Juan, Batangas. It is 10-15 minutes away from Acuatico, La Luz, and Palm beach. We send the instructions going to the beach house through text message. We have a guide to accompany the guests going there.
